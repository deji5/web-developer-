CONTENT

This word has two main meanings. The first has to do with being pleased and satisfied (feeling content) or making someone else feel happy and at peace with things (contenting them). The other meaning has to do with subject matter: the content of a history class might be American history. The content of a math class might be geometry. As long as there's a topic or subject, there's content.

CONTENT CREATION

Content creation is the strategic process of developing, producing, and sharing engaging forms of content—such as text, images, audio, and video—to inform, entertain, and connect with a specific target audience. This content serves to build brand awareness, foster customer loyalty, generate leads, and ultimately drive business goals by providing value and establishing thought leadership. It involves planning, ideation, creation, and promotion across various platforms like websites, social media, and blogs.
